There are so many great technologies out there to save the NHS time, money and most importantly deliver the best care. The problem is how do people find out what they are and what actually works? We are building the first review site for tried and tested medical products. These solutions are backed by hard evidence, reviews and expert curation.

We need to save our health service. The best way to do that is by embracing technology that allows the amaizing workers of the NHS to carry out their jobs in the best possible way, delivering personal, regular care. It is not about cutting corners, it is about making things better.

FAQ

Why dont we just cut services?
Because the NHS needs to improve and move forward not move backwards. Our health service is the biggest asset to the country.
Why are they not able to find their own tech?
Because the NHS has been chopped up into different authorities some better than others. This site helps the people in different places learn from each others trials and implementaions without going to the expense of running their own.
